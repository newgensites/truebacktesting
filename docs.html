How to run a backtest

  1. Open App
  2. Step or Play through candles
  3. Enter trades with SL/TP in R
  4. Let price hit SL/TP (auto close) or close manually
  5. Review Journal + Analytics

What “R” means

R is a risk multiple. If you risk $25 and make +2R, you can think of it as “+2 units” regardless of account size. This makes strategy results comparable across time.

  • Win = positive R
  • Loss = negative R
  • Expectancy = average R per trade

Backtest through TradingView

Use the built-in bridge on the App page to open a TradingView chart prefilled with your symbol and timeframe.

  1. Pick a symbol (e.g., OANDA:EURUSD) and timeframe (M5, H1, 1D) in the TradingView block.
  2. Click Open TradingView to launch a new tab with those values set.
  3. Turn on Bar Replay or Strategy Tester inside TradingView for real historical data and metrics.
  4. Keep entering and closing trades in BacktestLab so your journal and analytics stay in one place.

This flow keeps TradingView for data + charting and BacktestLab for execution journaling.
